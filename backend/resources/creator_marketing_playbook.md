Creator Marketing Playbook

- Start with one core promise and one target audience.
- Hook early with emotionally clear language.
- Adapt CTA by platform:
  - Instagram: short, visual-first CTA.
  - YouTube: subscribe/watch/follow-up CTA.
  - TikTok: direct action in one line.
- Keep captions concise and human.
- Match tone to persona (playful for young audiences, professional for B2B).
